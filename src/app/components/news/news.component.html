October 17, 2024

Daily Herald Endorses Penny Wegman

In the Nov. 5 election for Kane County Treasurer, Republican Gretchen Butler is challenging incumbent Democrat Penny Wegman. Wegman, seeking a second term, highlights her experience as county auditor since 2020 and her MBA. Butler emphasizes her 25 years of business experience, including managing finances in her own hardware store. Both candidates stress the need for independence in the auditor's role. Butler aims to cut costs and improve efficiency if elected. Wegman, during her term, improved transparency, launched new initiatives, and won an innovation award. While Butler is qualified, Wegman's proven record earns her the endorsement.

July 30, 2024

Candidate for Kane County Treasurer Penny Wegman receives international award

Candidate for Kane County Treasurer Penny Wegman recently received the Innovator Award from the International Association of Government Officials at the annual conference in New Orleans. The award recognized Wegman's work for the Kane County Procurement Card Program. Procurement cards – known as p-cards – are credit cards issued to department heads and office holders.
